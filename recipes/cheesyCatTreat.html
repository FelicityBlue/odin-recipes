Cheesy Cat Treat Recipes

Description

Calling all feline cheese connoisseurs! These cheesy delights will make your cat come running

Ingredients:

  • ¾ c. shredded cheddar cheese
  • ¾ c. whole-wheat flour
  • ¼ c. plain yogurt
  • ¼ c. cornmeal
  • 5 T. grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Combine ingredients into a dough
  2. Roll it out to about 1/4 inch
  3. Cut into one-inch pieces
  4. Bake on a greased cookie sheet at 350° for about 25 minutes
