Raw Chicken and Salmon Recipe

Description

This recipes is from Health,Home and Happiness. The recipe features raw chicken and salmon as its primary ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 700g raw chicken wings (bone-in, skin-on)
  • 100g raw salmon (with bone)
  • 100g raw chicken heart
  • 50g raw beef kidney
  • 50g raw chicken liver
  • 1 whole egg, raw (with shell)
  • 1 teaspoon taurine supplement
  • 2 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Cut the salmon and organ meats into chunks that will fit through your meat grinder.
  2. Divide the chicken wings as needed to fit through the grinder.
  3. Place a bowl under the grinder and feed the meat and organs through.
  4. Transfer the ground mixture to a large bowl then add the taurine, egg, and water to combine.
  5. Portion out the mixture and freeze or refrigerate.
